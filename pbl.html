Starting Tasks- You will want to write these down.

In this project you will create an travel agent itinerary (emaze.com) or a video (knovio.com) to evaluate three Mesoamerican societies.

You will start by learning about all 3 cultures. It would best for you to take notes. You are looking for the elements of SPRITE (Social, Political, Religion, Intellectual, Technological, Economic) and will incorporate these in your final project. Be sure your final submission has 10 total examples from any of these 6 categories.

  • Step 1: Go to page 6 of lesson 3.04 and complete the Review and Practice Activity. What did these three groups have in common?
  • Step 2: Go to page 3 of lesson 3.04 and review the infographic for the Inca. Ask yourself the following questions:
    1. What elements of SPRITE do you see in this civilization?
    2. What do you like MOST about this civilization? What would make you want to visit or move here?
    3. What did you like LEAST about this civilization? What would make you hesitant about visiting or living in this civilization?
